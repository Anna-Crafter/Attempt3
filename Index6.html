About

I am a hard working individual who is passionate about education, creativity, technology, people and the maker movement. I am currently a PhD student at the Queensland University of Technology, in the area of Human-Computer Interaction (HCI).
Within my research, I am focused on exploring how technology can be designed to engage older adults through their passions and interests. This includes understanding how their participation in making and crafting plays a role in their ageing experience, and how technology can support them.
